Yin Yang Breakout

Peace was never an option

Ying Yang Breakout is a dynamic Python game where the screen is split into two halves: one side with white blocks and the other with black blocks. A black ball starts on the white side, while a white ball starts on the black side.

Flow of the game

  • The objective is to turn the blocks to the opposite color
  • Each time a ball hits a block of the same color, the block changes to the opposing color.
  • The game becomes a battle between the two sides.
  • The goal of one team converting all blocks to their color to claim victory.
